About me.

I was born in Castro Valley, California and moved to Utah at age seven. and have lived in Spanish Fork and Cedar Hills. I graduated with honors and a 4.0 GPA from Lone Peak high school.

From 2009 to 2011, I performed service for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints in southern Germany, Austria, and Switzerland. I learned the German language and experienced the German culture.

I attended Brigham Young University from 2012 to 2018 and graduated with a bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering. I also received a minor in German and a minor Global Business and Literacy. I have continued my education by participating in classes and conferences. I am currently attending a coding bootcamp at the University of Utah.

I have spent the majority of my career in business and sales roles. From 2014 to 2018, I worked as a sales manager for New Star Communications, an At&t and DirecTv dealer. I led sales teams in Texas, Missouri, and Ohio. After graduating from BYU, I have combined my sales and engineering background in multiple technical sales roles. I currently work as a Sales Engineer for Moxtek, Inc. I married my wife Lyndsey on December 29th, 2018.
